ABOUT ME
My name is Elizabeth Demyanenko. I’m from Belarus, live in Minsk city. I’m the most fearless photographer I know. I have huevos of steel. Because I’m not afraid of rejection, looking dumb, and I know how to take a punch.
To just call yourself a photographer is putting yourself into a little box. If you see yourself as an artist, the heavens is the limit. You can fly, with your wings of creativity. And no, you will not die by flying too close to the sun.
EDUCATION
I was trained in the art of photography from an early age by my mother: her main outlet and profession was photography. Since childhood, I have studied the basics of composition, color theory, perspective and much more. Having grown up and gained experience with my friends and relatives, I graduated from the photography courses "Frame" and the School of photography at the university.
